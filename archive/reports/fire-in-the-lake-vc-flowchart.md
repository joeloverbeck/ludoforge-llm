# Fire in the Lake - VC Flowchart

**Status**: COMPLETED

## VC can Terror at Support?

### Yes

Terror Without exposing last Underground VC at any VC Base with no Support. Once VC at 0 Res, Tax before more Terror.

1) Everywhere where shift possible:
    a) highest Pop
2) If 12+ VC Res AND Aid < 30, Sabotage all unSabotaged LoCs

#### With

Tax In up to 4 spaces with Underground VC and no COIN Control.

1) if VC at 10+ Res, Subvert instead
2) On 2 then 1 Econ LoCs
3) At Active Support
4) Only if still at 0 Res
    1. At Active Opposition
    2. Randomly where able
    Within 3 & 4:
        a) the highest Pop
